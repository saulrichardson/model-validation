# Prior Review Note - Key Items Carried Forward to Q1 2026

## Context
This note summarizes items tracked from the prior CECL review cycle and their status in the Q1 2026 CECL Allowance Review package for Redwood Regional Bank.

## Items carried forward
### 1) Forecast horizon and reversion documentation vs. production behavior
- **Prior observation:** Governance materials described a fixed forecast horizon and linear reversion; reviewers requested explicit evidence that the reserve engine applies the same settings.
- **Q1 2026 status:** Item remains open pending final tie-out of engine configuration to the documented standard.
- **Impact:** Potential for differences in scenario responsiveness and quarter-over-quarter movement attribution.

### 2) Scenario ordering and directionality checks
- **Prior observation:** Segment-level scenario ordering controls were recommended to ensure severe losses are directionally higher than adverse absent a documented rationale.
- **Q1 2026 status:** Directionality checks were performed; one segment exhibits non-standard ordering under severe versus adverse and requires governance acknowledgement and documented explanation.

### 3) Qualitative overlay transparency
- **Prior observation:** Prior package requested clearer articulation of overlay rationale and better linkage to quantitative limitations.
- **Q1 2026 status:** Overlay rationale has been expanded; however, the committee requested that the memo explicitly distinguish between "fine-tuning" overlays and overlays that materially change segment outcomes.

## Recommended actions for Q1 2026 governance close
- Provide a configuration extract (or equivalent evidence) showing forecast and reversion quarters applied by the engine.
- Document the residential mortgage severe-vs-adverse directional outcome and whether it is considered acceptable.
- Confirm overlay governance expectations, including treatment when overlay amounts exceed documented thresholds.
