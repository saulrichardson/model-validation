# Monitoring Plan: Installment Lending Material Change

## Purpose
To describe the approach for ongoing monitoring of the recalibrated scorecard, ensuring performance and compliance with Cedar Retail Bank credit and risk policy standards.

## Key Monitoring Activities

### Monthly
- Total approval and decline counts (vs established baseline)
- Decline reason-code volume and distribution, by segment
- Portfolio delinquency and loss rate tracking
- Score distribution drift (applicant vs approve/decline)

### Quarterly
- Gini coefficient of approval segment
- Capture rate at threshold (0.39) and variance against monthly
- Thin file approval rate (pending formalized thresholds)
- Review of new feature stability: _low_cash_buffer_

### Annual
- Policy back-testing vs actual outcomes

## Reporting Thresholds
- Booking rate: deviation > ±3% from target triggers escalation
- Portfolio loss rate: deviation > ±0.5% from forecast triggers review
- Delinquency rate: deviation > ±0.3% vs baseline triggers investigation

> **Note:** Monitoring thresholds for thin-file approvals still require definition and will be formalized in a subsequent documentation update.
